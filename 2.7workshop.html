Three Natural Bridges

Three Natural Bridges (Tian Sheng San Qiao) National Geopark is a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site and one of the most beautiful natural attractions. It is located in Wulong County, Chongqing, China. Formed by the collapse of the earth’s crust, the area features massive sinkholes about 300–500 meters deep, with parts of the terrain creating natural archways that resemble bridges spanning between mountains.
